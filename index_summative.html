Clean Water and Sanitation

The problem

Clean water and sanitaion is a major problem all over the world, and there are many countries in the world that don't have clean water or sanitation. Many of these countries that are like this are known, but there are still parts of many developed countries that also don't have clean water or sanitation, such as Flint, Michigan. The main problem with this is that people need clean water for everyday activities. An example of this would be a village in Africa where there is no clean water to drink. These people are forced to drink dirty water which causes sickness, or drink no water and die from the extreme heat and exposure to the sun. The people of flint suffer similarly, because they don't have any clean water in their town, and are forced to connect to the Detroit water plant and have to run the water a long distance, which is very expensive for them.

What this site is about

This is a site to raise awareness around the fact that there are many people suffering in places like Africa, somewhere on Earth where water is most needed. This website is looking at existing data and analyze it which will bring awareness to the fact that these people are suffering. It is created for students in high school to try and inspire them. The site is mainly focusing on goal 6.4 - water usage and water usage efficiency. The site is focusing on reaching first world countries such as Australia, Canada, France, USA and others that have access to clean water and can in the future produce students that could have a big impact on the world and on this global issue. The site is targeting people who speak English, but is using many visual features to make sure people who speak different languages can understand the data the site is presenting and the problem at hand.
